# NEXUS-Cyber-Security-Platform
CyberShield + NEXUS is a unified, AI-powered cybersecurity platform that tackles two of South Africa’s most urgent cybersecurity challenges simultaneously: the critical skills shortage in cybersecurity talent, and the dangerous gap between attack detection and proactive threat response.

The platform has two integrated layers. NEXUS is a Narrative-Driven Autonomous Cyber Resilience Engine that continuously monitors organisational infrastructure, uses Graph Neural Networks and Large Language Models to understand cyberattacks as coherent narratives, predicts the attacker’s next move before it happens, and autonomously deploys defensive actions to stop threats in their tracks. CyberShield is the training and simulation layer that uses real NEXUS threat scenarios to train security analysts, IT staff, and students through an immersive gamified SOC (Security Operations Centre) experience powered by adaptive AI.

Together, NEXUS defends organisations while CyberShield builds the human skills to understand and respond to those threats — creating a complete cyber resilience ecosystem.
